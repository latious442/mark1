Skills & knowledge students should have


In this 21st century,the role of we,students,is becoming more and more important.This is because students have more opportunity & time to study things and knowledge across from the world.Therefore,we,students, come to be regarded as the lucky stars of the glorious future.In order to prove that their consideration is true ,we have to persist learning skills and knowledge which are related to the era of today. First and foremost,foreign language is the one that we should never ignore to learn.The reason is that almost all treasures of skills and knowledge are locked with the keystone of today: foreign language.To put it in a nutshell,books,online courses generally require a good command of foreign language -especially English.Even our daily-used gadgets :computer,laptop,I pad,etc, are associated with setting written in English.No matter how bad the grade of a student is, he can get acclaim ,chances to get scholar and chances to study abroad when he is skillful as well as talented in English. Secondly,since students of today are digital natives,it is knowledge about IT(Information Technology) that occupies an enormous part skill & knowledge student are supposed to have.A student who excels at using computer can earn money on his own way.What I mean is that,he can develop applications,games,websites and AI with the power of programming languages.Thereby,developing these things can offer him a wealth of money.That is why the ambition of some students is to become a developer.Moreover,if we,students,can make the best use of IT,the bright future will be arrived.Therefore,not surprisingly,Cs(computer science) major stands as the most popular course following the medical major even in Harvest university. At third,communication skill is one of the abilities students are supposed to have.Our world is getting narrow owing to advances in technology.Therefore,we may communicate to people from overseas.At that time,we need to have smooth-like-butter communication with the society all over the world .Therefore,most schools are going to plan to maximize this skill ;the term of EQ is becoming more and more popular ever than before. At last,we,students must beware of information related to science,health,energy consumption.In other words,students ought to have the wisdom of health for the sake of a happy atmosphere.Moreover,we should be aware of global warming,melting ice at the poles to create the ecstatic world. Overall,knowledge and skills are one of the mighty instruments to modify our world to be a better place that we dream of.Among a spectrum of people,students are the most effectual to handle those things.Described-above ones are the skills and knowledge students should have to the best of my grey matter. 10.2.24(14th ,also the last essay of my G-12 essay book)
